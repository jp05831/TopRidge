What We Build

The systems that keep everything running.

Growth creates demand. Conversion captures it. Operations makes sure nothing falls through the cracks — leads get followed up, data gets tracked, and everything stays connected as you scale.

Workflow Automation

Manual tasks slow you down and introduce errors. We build automations that handle the repetitive work — triggering actions, moving data between tools, and keeping processes running without anyone needing to babysit them.

  • Automated task triggers and sequences
  • Cross-platform workflow connections
  • Notification and alert systems
  • Process documentation and failsafe logic

Performance Dashboards

You shouldn't have to dig through five tools to know how things are going. We build dashboards that pull everything into one place — ad performance, lead flow, conversion rates, revenue — so you can make decisions in minutes, not hours.

  • Real-time KPI dashboards
  • Multi-source data aggregation
  • Custom reporting for your specific metrics
  • Scheduled reports and automated snapshots

CRM Setup & API Integrations

Your CRM should be the single source of truth for every lead and customer interaction. We set it up properly, connect it to every tool in your stack, and make sure data flows cleanly so nothing gets lost between systems.

  • CRM configuration and pipeline setup
  • Third-party API integrations
  • Data syncing across platforms
  • Custom fields, tags, and segmentation

Tracking Setup — Pixels & Events

If you can't track it, you can't improve it. We implement the full tracking layer — pixels, conversion events, UTM structures, and server-side tracking — so every click, form fill, and purchase is accounted for.

  • Meta and Google pixel installation
  • Custom conversion event configuration
  • Server-side tracking and CAPI setup
  • UTM strategy and attribution structure

Automated Follow-Up

Speed matters. When a lead comes in, the follow-up should happen immediately — not whenever someone gets around to it. We build email, SMS, and notification sequences that engage leads the moment they raise their hand.

  • Instant lead response sequences
  • Email and SMS drip campaigns
  • Re-engagement sequences for cold leads
  • Follow-up timing and cadence optimization

Lead Routing

The right lead needs to get to the right person at the right time. We build routing logic that assigns leads based on location, service type, availability, or any criteria that matters to your business.

  • Rule-based lead assignment
  • Round-robin and weighted distribution
  • Location and service-based routing
  • Instant notifications to assigned team members
Process

How we set it up.

We map out how your business actually runs, identify what's manual or broken, and build systems that handle it automatically — then keep improving as things change.

Map & Audit

We document your current tools, workflows, and data flow. Where are leads coming from? Where do they go? What's manual? What's breaking? We find every gap before building anything.

Build & Connect

We set up the CRM, wire the integrations, install tracking, build the automations, and configure the dashboards. Everything gets connected into one cohesive system.

Test & Launch

We run test leads through the entire system end-to-end — from ad click to CRM entry to follow-up sequence to team notification. Everything gets validated before going live.

Monitor & Optimize

Systems need maintenance. We monitor for issues, update automations as your business evolves, and continuously improve workflows based on what the data tells us.
